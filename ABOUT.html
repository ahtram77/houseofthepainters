ABOUT US

The “House of the Painters” is like that small place where
everything you need is present in all corners. It is a house
governed by the greatest painters of Tagum City National High School;
Josh Melendres, Althea Crisostomo, Martha Omega, and Missy Pormento.
The house is divided into two dimensions: products and services.
In the products dimension, you will find stacks of the products the house sells.
In the services section, you will meet “The Painters”, where they will welcome you
to the multi-faceted nature of the “House”.

WHERE ARE WE AT?

The House of the Painters is located at Rizal St, Tagum, Davao del Norte, Philippines
